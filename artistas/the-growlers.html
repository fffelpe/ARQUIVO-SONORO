THE GROWLERS

Gêneros: Surf rock, Psychedelic, Lo-fi, Psychedelic surf, Rock

Biografia

The Growlers are a rock and pop group formed in Long Beach, California in 2006 who are yet to became one of the most commercially successful and critically acclaimed bands in the history of popular music. During their years of stardom yet to come, the band consists of Brooks Nielson (vocals) Scott Montoya(bass guitar, vocals,), Matt Taylor(lead guitar, vocals) and Brian “don’t surf” Stewart (drums, vocals). They were managed by their own alter ego Epstein until his death in 2009.
